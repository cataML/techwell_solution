Our Story

At Techwell Cyber Café, we are more than just a place with computers and internet. We are a community hub in Nairobi, dedicated to making technology simple, fast, and accessible for everyone.

From helping you with government services (KRA, NTSA, HELB, eCitizen) to offering printing, scanning, data entry, gaming, and computer packages training, we provide all the digital solutions you need under one roof.
