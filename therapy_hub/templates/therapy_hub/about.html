Our Story

A Journey of Care and Purpose.

TechWell Therapy was founded by Erastus Ivelia and his wife, Catherine Ivelia, born from a shared commitment to mental wellness, compassion, and meaningful human connection.

What started as a heartfelt vision—to create a safe and supportive environment where individuals can heal, grow, and rediscover themselves—has steadily evolved into a trusted and respected counseling practice.

Guided by strong values and a passion for service, TechWell Therapy continues to expand its impact. With a dedicated team of qualified and experienced counselors, we are honoured to support individuals, couples, families, and organizations with professionalism, empathy, and unwavering excellence.

Our mission remains clear: to walk with you through every step of your healing journey, empowering you to live a healthier and more fulfilling life.
